Submission Process

Complete the form to submit your session proposal. If you have any additional screen shots or anything that won't fit into the form, feel free to email them as an an attachment to events-us@liferay.com. Just remind us in the email with which topic we should associate them.

Guidelines

We're looking for thought-provoking sessions that will help our attendees understand a cutting-edge concept or learn a new skill that will affect their business over the next 6 to 18 months. Help your proposal stand out in a good way by following the guidelines below:
  • Provide a simple and direct title.
  • Define your target audience. What are the topics and who are you trying to reach?
  • Share a unique story. Any insights, lessons, and personal experiences are well received by your audience.
  • Offer original and substantial content. Highlight the relevance of your topic.
  • Provide a clear and concise description of what attendees will learn.
  • Focus on experiences and insight instead of product or service benefits. Sales pitches will be rejected.
  • All presentations must be delivered in English. Submissions imply the willingness of the author to register and present the talk, if accepted.
